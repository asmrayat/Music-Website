Playlists are a great way to save collections of music, either for your own listening or to share. To create one: Tap Your Library. Tap CREATE. Give your playlist a name. Start adding songs (and we’ll help you along).

Tap Home. Tap Settings. Tap Data Saver. Switch on Data Saver.

Any playlist with the shuffle icon will play on shuffle. Some playlists won't have the shuffle icon, so you can tap any song to play it.
